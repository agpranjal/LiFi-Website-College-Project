We are a LiFi leader, turning imagination into reality

Back in 2005, we began our journey as a university lab. Following more than a decade of rigorous R&D and passion for innovation, we’ve quickly grown into a technology leader with global ambitions.

LightNet is a spin off research on Visible light communication that started in 2005. Today, with a growing team, powerful partnerships, an industry focus, and a solid portfolio of LiFi-focused patents and products, we’re setting our sights on the next technological revolution.

Our mission is to connect objects and people by transforming the 14 billion light points on the planet into a powerful and secure communication network.
